Eden Bakes Client Update Pack (v2)
----------------------------------
Copy these files into your repo under: restaurant-booking-app/client/

- index.html
- styles.css
- proxy.config.json (optional - allows `/api` calls to be rewritten to your server)
- (optional) eden-bakes-logo.png (upload your Eden Bakes logo and name it like this)

To enable the proxy in CodeSandbox or Vercel, replace <your-server-url> inside proxy.config.json
with your actual live API base URL.
